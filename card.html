Panda

Giant pandas will climb 13,000 feet (3,962 m) up the mountains of their home area to feed on higher slopes in the summer.

  • Scientific Name: Panda
  • Average Length: 5000 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 7 years
  • Habitat: China

Giant pandas are bears that are native to China, where they are considered a national treasure. Even with this exalted status, giant pandas are endangered.
